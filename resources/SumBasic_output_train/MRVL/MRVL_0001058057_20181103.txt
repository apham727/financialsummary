The Company’s fiscal year is the 52- or 53-week period ending on the Saturday closest to January 31.
Accordingly, every fifth or sixth fiscal year will have a 53-week period.
The additional week in a 53-week year is added to the fourth quarter, making such quarter consist of 14 weeks.
Fiscal 2018 had a 53-week year.
Fiscal 2019 is a 52-week year.
