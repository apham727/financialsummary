ITEM 2. Unregistered Sales of Equity Securities and Use of Proceeds. 
The following table contains information regarding our purchases of our common stock during the quarter. 
All open-market purchases during the quarter were made under the authorization from our board of directors to purchase up to $7.5 billion of additional shares of TI common stock announced September 17, 2015. On September 21, 2017, our board of directors authorized the purchase of an additional $6.0 billion of our common stock, and on September 20, 2018, our board of directors authorized the purchase of an additional $12.0 billion of our common stock. 
In addition to open-market purchases, 37,665 shares of common stock were surrendered by employees to satisfy tax withholding obligations in connection with the vesting of restricted stock units.
