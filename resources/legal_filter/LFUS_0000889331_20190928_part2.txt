Purchases of Equity Securities



The Company’s Board of Directors authorized the repurchase of up to 1,000,000 shares of the Company’s common stock under a program for the period May 1, 2018 to April 30, 2019 (the "2018 program").
As of April 30, 2019, there were 528,112 of authorized repurchases remaining under the 2018 program.
During the three and nine months ended September 28, 2019, the Company repurchased 311,786 and 579,916 shares of its common stock totaling $49.5 million and $95.0 million, respectively.
There are 500,000 shares yet to be purchased under the 2019 program as of September 28, 2019.
