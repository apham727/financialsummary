 On April 24, 2018, the Company’s Board of Directors authorized a new stock repurchase program under which the Company may purchase up to $2.0 billion of the Company’s Common Stock during the three-year period ending April 24, 2021 in accordance with the requirements of Rule 10b-18 of the Exchange Act (the “2018 Stock Repurchase Program”).
As of April 30, 2018, the Company repurchased approximately 0.7 million shares of its common stock for $55.5 million under the 2018 Stock Repurchase Program.
