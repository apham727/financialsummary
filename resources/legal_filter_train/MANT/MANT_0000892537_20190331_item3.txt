Item 3.Quantitative and Qualitative Disclosures about Market Risk
Our exposure to market risk relates to changes in interest rates for borrowing under our revolving credit facility.
At March 31, 2019, we had an outstanding balance of $96.0 million on our revolving credit facility.
A hypothetical 10% increase in interest rates would have a $0.1 million effect on our interest expense for the three months ended March 31, 2019.
Under this policy, no investment securities can have maturities exceeding six months and the weighted average maturity of the portfolio cannot exceed 60 days.
