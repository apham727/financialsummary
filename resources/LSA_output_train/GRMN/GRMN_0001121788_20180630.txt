The fitness segment revenues increase was primarily driven by strong sales in advanced wearables and cycling products.
Outdoor and aviation segment revenue increases were driven by increased sales across most product lines within each segment.
Cost of goods sold increased 7% in absolute dollars compared to the prior year quarter.
The fitness and aviation segments increase in cost of goods was largely consistent with the increase in sales.
Gross profit dollars in the second quarter of 2018 increased 8% while gross margin increased 30 basis points compared to the year-ago quarter.
Gross margin remained relatively flat across the aviation and fitness segments.
