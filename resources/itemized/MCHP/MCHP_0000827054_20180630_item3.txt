Item 3.
Quantitative and Qualitative Disclosures About Market Risk
As of June 30, 2018, our long-term debt (including current maturities) totaled $12.82 billion.
We have no interest rate exposure to rate changes on our fixed rate debt, which totaled $6.49 billion as of June 30, 2018.
We do have interest rate exposure with respect to the $6.33 billion balance of our variable interest rate debt outstanding as of June 30, 2018.
A 50 basis point increase in interest rates would impact our expected annual interest expense for the next 12 months by approximately $31.9 million.
