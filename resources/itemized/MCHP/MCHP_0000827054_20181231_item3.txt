Item 3.
Quantitative and Qualitative Disclosures About Market Risk

As of December 31, 2018, our long-term debt totaled $11.94 billion.
We have no interest rate exposure to rate changes on our fixed rate debt, which totaled $6.48 billion as of December 31, 2018.
We do have interest rate exposure with respect to the $5.46 billion balance of our variable interest rate debt outstanding as of December 31, 2018.
A 50 basis point increase in interest rates would impact our expected annual interest expense for the next 12 months by approximately $27.3 million.
