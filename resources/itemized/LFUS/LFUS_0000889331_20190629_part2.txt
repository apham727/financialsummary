Purchases of Equity Securities
The Company’s Board of Directors authorized the repurchase of up to 1,000,000 shares of the Company’s common stock under a program for the period May 1, 2018 to April 30, 2019 (the "2018 program").
On April 26, 2019, the Company's Board of Directors authorized a new program to repurchase up to 1,000,000 shares of the Company's common stock for the period May 1, 2019 to April 30, 2020 (the "2019 program").
As of April 30, 2019, there were 528,112 of authorized repurchases remaining under the 2018 program.
During the three and six months ended June 29, 2019, the Company repurchased 188,214 and 268,130 shares of its common stock totaling $32.0 million and $45.5 million.
There are 811,786 shares yet to be purchased under the program as of June 29, 2019.
