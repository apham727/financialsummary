ITEM 3.Quantitative and Qualitative Disclosures About Market Risk

Interest Rate Exposure

Based on the $1.1 billion of floating rate debt outstanding as of August 3, 2019, our annual interest expense would change by approximately $11.3 million for each 100 basis point increase in interest rates.
There were no other material changes in the nine-month period ended August 3, 2019 to the information provided under Item 7A.
