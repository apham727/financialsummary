Item 2.
Unregistered Sales of Equity Securities and Use of Proceeds  




The purchases include amounts that are attributable to shares surrendered to us by employees to satisfy, in connection with the vesting of restricted stock unit awards and the exercise of stock appreciation rights, their tax withholding obligations.
In June 2018, our Board of Directors authorized the repurchase of up to $350.0 million of our common stock as publicly announced in a press release issued on June 14, 2018.
As of February 28, 2019, the total amount authorized by the Board of Directors had been utilized.
