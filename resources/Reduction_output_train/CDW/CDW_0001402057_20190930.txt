Selling and administrative expenses increased $54 million, or 13.9%, to $445 million for the three months ended September 30, 2019, compared to $391 million for the three months ended September 30, 2018.

As a percentage of Net sales, selling and administrative expenses increased 20 basis points to 9.1% during the three months ended September 30, 2019, from 8.9% in the three months ended September 30, 2018.
